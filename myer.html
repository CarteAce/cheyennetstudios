Summary

Myer wants to be one of the first in transforming the in- store experience. To aid customers a built in AI mirror is introduced.

Problem

Bringing in a new customer by improving the in-store experience.

Interviews

Interviews were conducted with interviewees that have different backgrounds. These interviews were the main source of information and they provided me with a lot of insight.

Findings

Key information obtained included:


Majority of users surveyed said that they sometimes try on clothes/shoes before they make purchases.


More than 70% respondents said they spend less than $250


Majority of respondents said they just grab what they want and go (%60)


Majority of the surveys stated that people mostly use their footwear for casual. Some just use for leisure, sports and work.


Concerns & Problems

The main concerns and problems mentioned by the participants.


Too many unavailable cashiers


Narrow aisle/space


Long queue to get into fitting room/checkout

Personas

There were two personas made for each of the major user groups. The personas were created with the information collected from the interviews

Summary

This application was made to aid Australians with the ability to be able to grow and cook their own produce.

Problem

People experience a significant disconnect between their food production and consumption.

Interviews

Interviews were conducted with interviewees that have different backgrounds. These interviews were the main source of information and they provided me with a lot of insight.

Key Quotes

“Starting out to plant produce is hard”


“I think gardening is fun and I would like to do it properly”


“Would like to learn more about gardening”


“I don’t want to start planting”


Findings

Gardening should be introduced as fun and shouldn’t take too much time and effort


The information given should be thorough and that there should be some level of satisfaction


The more useful information about the plant would be best


Growing your own produce should be deemed as open to everyone

Personas

There were four personas made for each of the major user groups. The personas were created with the information collected from the interviews

Summary

Walks Wags Weather is an application that is to help dog owners/walkers find routes to walk their dogs.

Problem

Dog owners are bored of the same routes and routines while walking with their dog.

Interviews

Interviews were conducted with interviewees that have different backgrounds. These interviews were the main source of information and they provided me with a lot of insight.

Findings

Key information obtained included:


40% of users check the weather daily


Most of the participants are fairly tech savvy


Most of the participants walk their dogs daily

Recommendations

Reccomendations from the information obtained included


Incorporation of a more personalised map


The incorporation of social media functions into the application


An automatic fitness tracking element

Personas

There were four personas made for each of the major user groups. The personas were created with the information collected from the interviews
